Project goal

The goal of our project is to ease the stress of UST students.

Project prototype

Learning

Project 2 further develop my skill of prototyping.
By designing different prototypes, the optimal one can be found.

Our team found that many of the students in HKUST are stressing out.

Therefore, our team proposed to create a chatbot to provide emotional support to our fellow UST students.

reflection

Through this project, I gained the knowledge of the process to create an working prototype from an ideated idea.
After creating a prototype, it is important to make sure that the user is comfortable to use the product and provides actual help to the user.
If more data is collected, the chatbot can potentially have a larger user group outside UST.
